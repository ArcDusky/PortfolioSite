Gameplay features

  • A retro-futuristic 3D buggy-racing game.
  • 30 race tracks. Race against computer-racers on "Normal" or "Easy" difficulty.
    • 5 Havok tracks. Dangerous tracks designed for buggy-collisions. Avoid the wreckage, survive, and win!
    • 5 Super tracks. Races with upgraded hyperdrive buggies.
    • 3 bonus tracks. Silly races, such as tiny toy-buggies on a giant pool table, or shopping carts racing in a supermarket.
  • 23 puzzle-skill-challenge tracks. Navigate obstacles, find all of the checkpoints, or figure out how to reach the finish line.
  • Time trial mode, which records your best lap time as a ghost, and allows you to race alongside it or watch its replay.
  • Every level has a "fast lap time" that I've challenged you to beat.
  • Every level has my best lap time (as a ghost) that I bet you cannot beat.
  • Every level has an orange flag hidden somewhere to collect.
  • Buggy damage! Panels fall off, wheels bend, and the buggies might explode!
  • Local multiplayer (split-screen) with 2 to 4 players. Race against each other, or cooperate to dominate the computer racers.
  • Spectate mode, to watch the race from a controllable sky-camera.
  • Tour cameras, to automatically show you around the levels.
  • Minimaps in the corner, showing the shape of the course, and where all of the buggies are.
  • Buggy painting. Choose any color for your buggy's body-panels and body-frame.
  • Buggy drivers. Choose from 26 wacky characters to sit in your buggy's cockpit.
  • 3 different driving cameras. (1) following camera, (2) strict following camera, for going upsidedown, and (3) cockpit camera. Each with a rear-view camera.
  • GameJolt integration. There are extra features if you sign in with your GameJolt account.
    • Earn trophies / achievements for completing specific tasks within the game.
    • Time trial leaderboards for every track, so you can compete with other players for the best lap times.

Technical features

  • Designed to run well on low-performance computers. Options for resolution, fullscreen mode, and overall graphics quality (low, normal, high).
  • Simple controls, so it's easy to learn.
  • Customizable controls, and gamepad support.
  • Options for sound and music volume.
  • Option to silence engine-noise (if they get annoying).
  • Option to toggle horizontal or vertical split-screen (during multiplayer).
  • Option to increase game speed up to 150% (if the game feels slow).
