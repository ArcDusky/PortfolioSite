ArcDusky's Chess

  • User controls both players (there is no automated opponent).
  • Fully functional.
    • Enforces legal piece movement.
    • Allows castling.
    • Allows en passant pawn attacks.
    • Detects checks and checkmates.
  • "Threats" button shows squares that enemy can attack (in red).
  • "Moves" button shows which of your pieces can move (in green). While holding a piece, it shows which moves that piece can make.
  • "View" button toggles between the 3D view and a top-down 2D view.
