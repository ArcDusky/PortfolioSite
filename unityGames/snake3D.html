ArcDusky's Snake 3D

  • The snake is always moving forward. Use the arrow keys to steer the snake's head.
  • Eat the apples to score. But with each one, the snake will grow longer and move faster.
  • Don't collide with anything (walls or your snake's body).
  • Classic mode has walls around the area.
  • Wrap mode has a defined area, but no walls. As the snake's head leaves the area, it is "teleported" to the opposite side.
